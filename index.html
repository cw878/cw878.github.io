About

Built on hands-on renewables delivery, not just advisory theory.

Founder

Cailong "Phoenix" Wang

  • Head of Renewables, Mycelium (Greek IPP)
  • Prior roles: Gulermak, Samba Solar, PowerChina
  • Origination · Due diligence · EPC · Asset management

Phoenix CleanTech exists because public bodies are being asked to deliver net zero outcomes with finance, procurement and technical expertise they often can't justify hiring in-house.

Phoenix CleanTech Ltd was founded to close that gap — bringing private-sector renewables and finance discipline to councils, housing associations and community energy groups.

The practice is led by Cailong Wang, who leads solar, onshore wind and battery storage strategy as Head of Renewables at Mycelium, a Greek independent power producer. His background spans operational roles at Gulermak, Samba Solar and PowerChina covering origination, due diligence, EPC delivery, asset management and transactions.

That combination — capital markets fluency and on-the-ground project delivery — is what's applied to every engagement, whether it's a funding strategy for a council retrofit programme or the launch of a community energy club.
